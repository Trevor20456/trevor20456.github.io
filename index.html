Professional Introduction

About Me

I am beginning my role as the 7–12 social science teacher at Trona High School, where I will teach middle and high school history, government and economics. Before coming to Trona, I completed a long-term teaching assignment at Covina High School, teaching Economics/Government, Psychology and AVID. I earned my bachelor’s degree in history and hold a California Single Subject Teaching Credential in Social Science. I am also completing the Master of Science in Educational Technology program at California State University, Fullerton.
