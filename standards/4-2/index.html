ISTE Coach Standard 4.2

Connected Learner

Coaches model the ISTE Standards for Students and the ISTE Standards for Educators, and identify ways to improve their coaching practice.

Review the complete official standard ↗

Standard overview

Indicators

Plain-language summaries are provided below. The official ISTE page remains the source of record.

4.2.a

Deepen expertise

Pursue professional learning that strengthens knowledge of the ISTE Standards.

4.2.b

Learn through networks

Participate in professional learning networks and stay current with technology and learning research.

4.2.c

Improve continuously

Set shared goals, reflect on results and make purposeful improvements.

Supporting evidence

Continued Professional Learning

I am building my coaching practice by pursuing professional learning that connects emerging technology to real classroom decisions.

Professional learning plan

Professional Learning Evidence in Progress

I will add documentation and a reflection after completing a professional-learning experience that connects emerging technology to my coaching practice.

Growth in progress

Next evidence
