Technology Professional Development Plan

AI Literacy for Secondary Classrooms

A practical professional learning plan that helps educators question AI-generated information, examine bias and privacy and design one classroom activity that keeps human judgment at the center.

OverviewPlanOnline PD ModuleAdditional Resources

Plan at a glance

Built for a Small Secondary Staff

The plan begins with questions teachers and students already face—accuracy, bias, privacy and responsible use—rather than presenting AI as one more tool everyone must adopt.

Audience

Grades 7–12 educators

Teachers working across content areas in a small rural secondary setting.

Need

Shared language for responsible AI

A practical way to address verification, bias, privacy and classroom expectations.

Outcome

One classroom-ready AI literacy activity

Each educator adapts a current lesson so students question, verify or evaluate AI-generated content.
