Technology Professional Development Plan

Plan

This plan moves from a shared understanding of AI literacy to classroom design, peer feedback and reflection on what students actually need.

OverviewPlanOnline PD ModuleAdditional Resources

Context and need

Why This Professional Learning?

Teachers and students are already encountering AI-generated text, images and recommendations, but familiarity does not guarantee literacy. Educators need time to examine accuracy, bias, privacy and responsible use in the context of lessons they already teach.

Learning objective

What Teachers Will Create

By the end of the initial session, educators will adapt one current lesson into a short AI literacy activity in which students verify a claim, evaluate a consequence or explain a responsible-use decision using credible evidence.

Three-month scope and sequence

Learn, Design, Reflect

  1. September

    Build Shared Foundations

    Explore responsible AI use, bias, source evaluation and privacy through selected AI 101 for Teachers resources.

  2. October

    Design and Try

    Adapt one classroom lesson, review it for accessibility and use it with students when appropriate.

  3. November

    Reflect and Improve

    Examine student work and teacher observations, then revise the activity and identify one next step for classroom practice.

Two-hour initial session

Session Outline

10 minWelcome and purpose

Surface the AI questions educators are already hearing from students.

20 minAI literacy foundations

Review human responsibility, verification, bias and privacy.

20 minExperience an activity

Compare an AI-generated historical summary with credible sources.

35 minDesign

Adapt one lesson using a verify, evaluate or responsible-use pathway.

15 minAccessibility and privacy check

Remove barriers and avoid unnecessary collection of personal information.

15 minPeer feedback

Check alignment, clarity and whether student thinking remains central.

5 minCommit

Name when the activity will be used and what evidence will inform revision.
