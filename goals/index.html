Professional Direction

Goals and Future Plans

My goals center on building a strong classroom foundation first and then using that experience to support practical, equitable technology use across a school community.

Growth pathway

From Classroom Practice to Technology Leadership

2026–27

Establish Consistent Classroom Systems

Create manageable routines across five social science courses, use formative feedback to adjust instruction and teach source evaluation, digital citizenship and responsible AI use directly.

Graduate Program

Complete and Apply the Professional Learning Plan

Develop the portfolio and Technology Professional Development Plan into resources that respond to a real staff need and can be improved through feedback and evidence.

Long Term

Support Colleagues Without Adding Complexity

Grow into a teacher-leader who can help colleagues choose technology based on instructional purpose, learner access and realistic classroom implementation.

“Technology should make a meaningful part of teaching or learning clearer, more accessible or more useful. If it does not, the tool should not be the focus.”

— Guiding principle for continued growth
